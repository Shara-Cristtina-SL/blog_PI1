What is The Ruins of Self ?

Ruins of Self is a concept. The concept of what makes us, us. The idea that we are controlled by the feelings inside us… they are what drives us through our lives. All the decisions we make, all the paths we take are, from the beginning of our lives, made from what we feel. The profession you choose, you choose because you think is right! But what makes you think it is right ? The feeling. You may feel it gives you pleasure; You may feel it gives you comfort. You may feel it gives you security, but in the end, it was all because of your feelings.

Even if you don’t feel good about something you made, if you go inside and think about it, you will find a core feeling that made that decision (empathy, for example, is a feeling that makes you make a decision because it will be good for someone else other than you), as all things everyone does in life.
